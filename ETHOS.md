# Ethos

## What This Project Believes

1. **AI editors should feel like human editors.** The goal is not automation for its own sake — it's giving creators a collaborator who understands their vision and executes it professionally.

2. **Quality over speed.** A video that takes 10 minutes longer to process but comes out polished is worth more than a rushed edit. Every pipeline step has validation gates.

3. **Evidence over opinion.** Every editing decision is backed by data — transcript analysis, silence detection, content ratings. No guessing.

4. **The creator has final say.** AI makes recommendations, humans approve. Nothing publishes without explicit approval (unless auto-publish is deliberately enabled).

5. **Cost transparency.** Creators should know exactly what each video costs to process (~$0.15-0.25). No hidden API calls or runaway spending.

6. **Self-healing systems.** When something breaks, the system should try to fix itself before escalating. Error patterns are tracked, learned from, and propagated.

7. **Compound improvement.** Every video processed makes the system smarter. Observations feed insights, insights feed evolution.
